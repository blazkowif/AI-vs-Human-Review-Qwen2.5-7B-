# AI-vs-Human-Review-Qwen2.5-7B-
This dataset contain Human and AI reviews. Human-written reviews were taken from the Amazon Reviews dataset by Ni et al. due to its historical nature. AI-generated reviews were produced by paraphrasing using Qwen 2.5-7B and Gemini 2.5, inspired by Salminen et al. (2022), to support comparison across AI generations.
